China will come up with its own smart car
standards, traffic rules, laws and regulations
relating to safety, according to the China
Industry Innovation Alliance for the Intelli-
gent and Connected Vehicles.

Li Jun, a China Academy
of Engineering academician
and also director of the
alliance's experts' committee,
said that China will also
launch design guidance for
smart cars, smart map
architecture and standards
for test grounds.

Li said the smart car industry
is able to enlarge the
current car industry by
1 trillion yuan ($158.15
billion) and also able
to boost fast
development in 5G, internet of
cars, big data, artificial
intelligence and new-
energy vehicles.

Zhang Junyi, a partner with NIO
Capital, said that the smart
car is the trend
for the car industry
and China's auto sector
will usher in a new
key development stage with
the improvement of infras-
tructure, technology, policies
and laws.
